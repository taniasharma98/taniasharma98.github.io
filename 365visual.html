Role

Visual Artist, Graphic Designer

High Frequency Output

On March 03, 2019 I joined Futureland. Futureland is a new way to learn anything. It is a set of tools designed to help you learn a new skill by creating something everyday. Currently, Futureland consists of a community of self learners, and has offered me a profile that documents my process.

When I first joined, I wanted to experiment with a medium that would improve my creative skills. I had a desire to improve my visual design/graphic design skills, therefore, I purchased an iPad Air and have been drawing on the Procreate app since. Throughout this process, I have learned a lot about what digital art can be and I have also practised self-discipline by creating and sharing everyday. This method is very effective for someone who wants to learn a skill and wants to develop tremendous amounts of output.
